Baked Potato Breakfast Hash Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 baked potato, diced
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 2-3 slices of Canadian bacon, chopped
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add diced onions and chopped Canadian bacon and saut√© for 3-4 minutes or until softened.
  3. Add diced potatoes and continue to cook until potatoes are crispy and browned.
  4. Season with garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  5. Crack eggs into the skillet and cook until whites are set but yolks are still runny.
  6. Top the potato hash with shredded cheese and cover the skillet for 1-2 minutes, or until cheese is melted.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
